Welcome to the official Coldplay website. Formed in London in 1996, Coldplay has captivated global audiences with evocative melodies and heartfelt lyrics. Fronted by lead vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, and featuring guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, and drummer Will Champion, the band gained widespread recognition with their debut album "Parachutes" in 2000, featuring the breakthrough single "Yellow." Known for an atmospheric sound that blends alternative rock, pop, and electronic influences, subsequent albums like "A Rush of Blood to the Head" and "Viva la Vida or Death and All His Friends" solidified Coldplay's status as one of the world's best-selling music acts. Renowned for vibrant energy and visual artistry, our live performances often feature elaborate light shows and interactive elements. Throughout our career, we've received numerous awards and accolades, continuously evolving our style while maintaining a core message of love, hope, and human connection.
